ABOUT US

Welcome to Spirit Yoga! We are a team of driven mountainpreneurs who are passionate about yoga. We cultivate a welcoming space and exceptional teachers to facilitate your growth, so you can be sure that the time you spend on your mat is a gift you give to the world.
